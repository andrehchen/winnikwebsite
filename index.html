01 / Research

Polymer science, self-assembly & nanochemistry

We like problems that require a combination of synthesis and physical measurements.

View research →

The Winnik group studies a broad spectrum of topics in the general areas of polymer science, self-assembly, and nanochemistry.

Our projects include:

  1. Mass cytometry with mesoporous silica nanoparticles & metal chelating polymers
  2. Polymer chemistry in latex films
  3. Rod-like nanoparticles and cellulose nanocrystals (CNCs)

02 / Winnik

Mitch Winnik, Principal Investigator

Professor Winnik began his research career and obtained tenure as an organic chemist. His transformation into a polymer chemist, which began in the late 1970s, taught him that a change of field or a change of research topic can lead to growth in creativity.
